KOHO team.

Juliet is big enough to have deep bench strength but small enough to ensure you’ll always have access to the entire leadership team. Below is a snapshot of your day-to-day team. Made up of a mix of characters, backgrounds, experiences and perspectives. (Rollover bio pics to say hi!)

Denise Co-Founder, Creative Director

Denise cut her teeth in advertising working alongside creative giants at Cutwater in San Francisco. Her time there shaped her approach to advertising: Make work you would proudly put on your wall in your home. With that goal in mind, she strives to turn ads into work that is beloved by the target it is designed to reach, and in doing so, connecting on a much more meaningful level. In addition to her day job, Denise teaches at Miami Ad School to help young creatives learn the ropes of  the industry. She is also a board member of the ADCC.

Mat Head of Strategy

Mat’s international experience has guided brands such as Nissan, Infiniti, Vodafone and Coca-Cola. In Canada he’s worked across most industries including FI, CPG, auto, QSR, tech and the fast-moving cannabis category. Beginning his career in account management with TBWA in the UK, with stints at Isobar Canada, and Movement London, Mat evolved his strategic planning expertise at John St and Grip Limited. He’s a firm believer in finding an authentic values-exchange between people and brands, with an approach to building long-term equity for his clients. Mat is an accredited research moderator through the Canadian Marketing Research and Intelligence Agency.

Julie Business Driver

Julie has been in advertising for just over 10 years, working across a wide range of business verticals including automotive, beer, wine, and spirits, banking, cannabis, restaurants, homewares, grocery, and more. Basically, all the things that make our lives better. Her education background in creative advertising and psychology fuels her desire to put work into the world that is thoughtful, empathetic, and beautifully designed. However, in true Business Driver fashion, she also gets deep satisfaction from a solicitously organized excel sheet. I guess there is, indeed, beauty all around us.

Kira Head of Connections Strategy

Kira is a consumer experience fanatic, with a passion for leading brands to what’s next at the intersect of people, platforms, and culture. She has a unique mix of experience leading strategy teams both creative and media side, where she’s contributed to award winning work for Chevrolet, Coca-Cola, and Aurora Cannabis. At age 30, Kira was named VP of Strategy at one of the country’s largest media agencies – making her the youngest strategy head in a global network of over 45,000. But a burning desire to be back at the creative table, influencing ideas at inception and across the full consumer journey, led her back to her roots.

Ranz Digital Media Lead

Considering herself a renaissance woman in the media industry, Ranzel has over 5 years of experience in 360 media planning and buying on both online and offline channels, an expert in digital performance marketing, and a love for data analytics. She gained her experience “playing at big courts (agencies)” such as Publicis Media, Starcom and PHD. Her background includes working on banking behemoths such as TD Bank and Scotiabank. She has also steered media for smaller accounts such as The Source, Pizzaiolo and LCBO.

Schulze Head of Production

Andrew has been in advertising for over 30 years having produced over 1000 communications for TV, radio, web, mobile, in-flight, elevator, stadiums, live events and more. Maria Art Director

People before platforms

People before platforms

At Juliet, our strategic process doesn’t stop at unlocking a pivotal proposition for your brand. As creative thinking ramps up and before Media Planning kicks off, a team of Connections Planners step into the shoes and scrolls of your audience. There, they bring the same strategic rigour that was applied to ‘what’ your brand says to ‘when and where’ your brand will say it. Turning over every stone across the decision journey to unearth the most effective and cut-through contexts for your communications.

Nothing left to fate

Nothing left to fate

We once heard it said, “the media will sort itself out.” We respectfully disagree. It’s why the discovery led by our Connections team lands in a Connections Playbook. Some clients have been known to call it, ‘the missing link’. A tightly choreographed play-by-play for how creative and channels come together across the full decision journey, and as a result the conversion funnel. An integrated plan of attack that gets all the parts working harder as a whole—from filling the funnel, to feeding performance tactics.

Better conversations, better buys

Better conversations, better buys

Media Planning conversations often feel like they require an enigma machine to decode. We do away with that. Instead of talking about how we’re going to chase down the lowest rate, you’ll find our Media Planning team focused on business and marketing outcomes. After all, last we checked a CPM wasn’t a business metric, and there’s a distinct difference between quality reach and cheap reach.

Always-ready > Always-On

Always-ready > Always-On

We don’t carry the dead weight and process burdens of a big shop. Which means, once your campaign is in market, we’re better positioned to respond. Final buys are mapped to measurement plans that tell the whole story (not only happy endings), and data is piped for performance pass-back. From there, weekly reports keep all teams – including creative, and production – at the ready to respond and iterate to real-time campaign, cultural, and market insight.
